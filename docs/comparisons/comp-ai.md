# Efterlev vs Comp AI

Comp AI (trycompai) is an OSS AI-agent-driven compliance platform covering SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and FedRAMP across one product. 600+ customers, SaaS-first.

Stub for SPEC-38.12. Short version:

- **Comp AI is multi-framework, breadth-focused.** SOC 2 first; FedRAMP at ~41% in their own demo screenshots, framed in legacy Rev 5 terms.
- **Efterlev is FedRAMP-first, depth-focused.** KSI-native, 80%+ FRMR-Moderate coverage as the v1 goal.
- **Different buyers.** Compliance team at a SaaS doing SOC 2 + ISO 27001 picks Comp AI. DevSecOps lead at a SaaS pursuing first FedRAMP authorization picks Efterlev.
